## Chosen Approach: Editorial Mono

**Design Movement:** Swiss editorial / International Typographic Style, adapted for a modern creative studio.

**Core Principles:**
1. **Grid discipline** — every element snaps to a 12-column grid with consistent gutters; alignment is visible and intentional.
2. **Typographic hierarchy** — display headlines at massive scale, body at comfortable reading size, monospace for metadata and labels.
3. **Light-dominant with dark rhythm** — predominantly white/off-white, broken by full-bleed near-black sections that create deliberate contrast and pacing.
4. **Restraint** — one accent color used sparingly; no decorative gradients; motion is subtle and purposeful.

**Color Philosophy:**
- **Base:** Off-white `#FAFAF9` (warm paper) and near-black `#0D0D0D` for dark sections.
- **Accent:** Saturated electric blue `#1D4ED8` — used only for links, CTAs, key emphasis, and hover states. Communicates trust, precision, and technical capability.
- **Muted text:** `#737373` for secondary text on light, `#A3A3A3` on dark.
- **Hairlines:** `#E5E5E5` on light, `#262626` on dark.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric editorial grid. Left-aligned content with intentional negative space. Section indices (01, 02, 03) in monospace on the left margin. Dark sections break rhythm at key moments — the "how we work" process and the proof/stats band.

**Signature Elements:**
1. **Monospace section indices** — `01 / SERVICES` style labels in JetBrains Mono.
2. **Spotlight motif** — a subtle light-beam gradient at the top of dark sections, echoing the Lumet logo.
3. **Hairline dividers** — thin horizontal rules between content blocks, not boxes or cards with shadows.

**Interaction Philosophy:** Quiet confidence. Hover states are minimal — underline reveals, slight color shifts. No bouncing, no flashy transitions. The site feels like it was built by people who pay attention to details.

**Animation:** Fade-up on scroll (opacity 0 → 1, translateY 12px → 0, 600ms ease-out). Staggered by 80ms per item in groups. Magnetic CTA buttons with subtle translate on mouse proximity. Section transitions are instant — no scroll-jacking.

**Typography System:**
- **Display:** Inter, weight 600–700, tight tracking (-0.03em), sizes 3rem–6rem.
- **Body:** Inter, weight 400, 1.125rem, line-height 1.7, color muted.
- **Mono:** JetBrains Mono, weight 400–500, 0.75rem–0.875rem, uppercase for labels, tracking 0.05em.
- **Headings:** Never centered except hero. Left-aligned with grid discipline.

**Brand Essence:** The studio behind the studios — white-label fulfillment for agencies that demand senior-level execution. Personality: precise, reliable, quietly excellent.

**Brand Voice:** Short, confident sentences. No hype. Sound like a studio that does the work, not talks about it.
- Headline: "You sell it. We build it."
- CTA: "Book a call"
- Ban: "Welcome to our website", "Get started today", "Unlock your potential"

**Wordmark & Logo:** "Lumet." wordmark with spotlight beam — provided by client. White version for dark backgrounds, black version for light.

**Signature Brand Color:** Electric blue `#1D4ED8` — ownable, technical, trustworthy.
